Record Link is an upcoming service to allow every one of your closest friends, your family members, or any one, to share music whatever the platform you use. Whether it's Spotify® or Apple Music®.

Share playlists and collaborate all together, whatever platform you use. Record Link does the rest for you, by synchronising everyone's playlist through your Spotify® or Apple Music® account, only if you authorized your music service of choice to be linked with Record Link.

Share songs or albums using a Record Link URL to instantly redirect your recipient to their platform of choice.
