Fraca

Merge borders with swift conversions and calculations for French people going to Canada, or Canadians going to France.

Money on the Line
Transform Euros to Canadian dollars and Euros to Canadian dollars freely, and quickly. Fraca also includes a Tax Calculator to calculate the actual amount of Canadian dollars you'll pay depending on the selected province, with a comparison in Euros.
Universal Conversions
Units are the worst things in the world, and we know you hate it too. Fraca simplifies conversions with instant calculations and the conversion rate shown at the bottom.
Canada-full
Fraca is available for everyone in Canada, whatever province or time zone you live in! It instantly changes the time and/or taxes according to your changes, without needing to restart the app. Making this the best tool for French travelers.
Everywhere, everytime
Fraca is a SwiftUI app, made for all types of Apple devices, wether it's an iPhone, an iPad, an Apple Watch, a Mac, or even an Apple Vision Pro. It's also available in English, and in French, at all times.
