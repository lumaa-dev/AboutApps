Bubble

Pop in the simplest Mastodon client ever, for iOS.

100% writer
When writing or replying to a post, nothing will appear on your screen other than your text. So you can focus only on what you write.
For everyone
Whether you are visually impaired, or hard of hearing, Bubble takes it all in charge thanks to iOS.
More organized
Your notifications are stacked by type, and by date. Get details on who interacted by tapping the gray circle.
Top Toot
The Discovery page helps you keep track of the current events, the people you should follow, and the hit toots.
Keep an eye out
Search for posts, hashtags, people, and everything... Results are always sorted, and you can also sort them out yourself.
All this in 5 tabs
Bubble has the same number of tabs as fingers on a human hand, as well as all the previous features mentionned previously.
Plus
Also called Bubble+, Plus grants you access to so many more features! Like the Content Filter, saving attachments, a full access to the Intelligent Feed, and so much more to come...
