I remember having the idea of making my own music streaming service, and I have partially done it! And YOU can run it yourself! This iOS and tvOS app replicates the Apple Music app, but anyone could make an Artisticly app. This one is the official Artisticly app, fully integrated with the operating system.


You can also find a web version of Artisticly for all unsupported devices, or if you simply want a temporary look at a library. It is built using Vue.js and it is also a web app, meaning you can add it to your macOS dock, iPhone home screen, Windows desktop, or anything!
