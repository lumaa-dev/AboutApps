By using TicTac, you can record your reminders with various levels of importance. There is also a notification feature that alerts you several hours in advance, turning your reminder into a live activity and in your Dynamic Island. You can mark it as completed at any time of the day, either from the application or through the reminders widget.

You will also find counters that counts down in real-time from the current date to a date of your choice. You can associate an icon that best represents the event, visible on your home and lock screens thanks to the counters widget. Additionally, you can display a counter in your Dynamic Island and view it as a live activity on your lock screen.

Finally, organize your tasks from the application by selecting a descriptive icon for each task. You have the option to designate tasks as essential to never forget them.
