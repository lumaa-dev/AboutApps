Remote 3.1.0

Introducing Liquid Glass to Cider Remote, as well as playlists in the Library Browser. And lots of other improvements. Available now for all iOS 17+ iPhones and iPads.

Features

Can we add some more color?

iOS 26 introduces Apple's new design language: Liquid Glass. Beautifully integrated while also keeping the traditional style.

I'll keep your best interest

View and play songs from albums and playlists directly from your Apple Music library through Cider Remote.

You are all I need

Sing the lyrics on-screen of your currently playing song, thanks to Apple Music & Musixmatch's lyrics

You wanna turn this sh*t out

Lay your iPhone on the side, with Cider Remote open, to activate the Horizontal Layout, featuring Immersive Lyrics.

You are here!

View the current track everywhere, any time on your lock screen and Dynamic Island with the option to play and pause.

It goes faster and faster

Using iOS 18, you can add a Cider Remote action to toggle playback in an instant, in the Control Center, lock screen or Action button.

Sink yourself into me

Manage your queue by sliding off tracks or moving songs around.
