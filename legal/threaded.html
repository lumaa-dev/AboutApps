Explanation

On September 10th, 2024, Instagram's intellectual property counsel contacted Lumaa to discuss the Threaded app, which may cause confusion with Instagram's trademarks. Even if Threaded is independently owned and not affiliated with Instagram, a name change was necessary to prevent legal issues against Threaded and Lumaa.

The full rebranding took an entire day to do, from naming the app to making a new icon. From Threaded to Bubble. Learn more about Bubble here or view Bubble in the App Store. The rebranding came as a app update on September 15th, 2024 in the 1.2.0 update.

November Update

On November 6th, 2024, Instagram's intellectual property counsel replied to Lumaa's email. They are appreciative of the new branding... But they were not very happy of the old screenshots left on the website. The necessary change was made under 24 hours, providing new screenshots.
