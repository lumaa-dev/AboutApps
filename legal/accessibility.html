What is it?

Apple recently introduced the Accessibility Nutrition Labels in the App Store for users to learn more about available accessibility features in apps like shown below. While this allows people with disabilities to choose better the apps they use, it is important to note that these labels are not a guarantee of accessibility.

Lumaa's Apps

All accessibility features displayed on Lumaa's apps are not guaranteed to be working, they may be incomplete or not fully functional. Reasons are that Apple automatically provides some of these features thanks to their frameworks and tools, and some features may not be available in all languages. For more information, please refer to Apple's website.
