History

Lumaa's relationship with the owners of the Cider app is particular. It started with a few simple Pull Requests on the Cider Remote repository. After an unsuccessful email sent to Cider Collective, developer Harshil "Deadfrost" asked for a specific feature to be implemented in Cider Remote, which is the song search bar when viewing the queue.

Nothing to Something

After a successful feature being done. On February 14th, 2025, Elijah "cryptofyre" gave Lumaa a full access to Cider Remote, including for publishing new versions of the Remote app. Making Lumaa an indirect volunteer at Cider Collective.

Important Note

Even if Lumaa has access to the entire Cider Remote project, Lumaa doesn't work with Cider Collective for features, bug fixes, and etc... Lumaa doesn't earn money from Cider Collective's projects and Lumaa doesn't get directions from Cider Collective for Cider Remote's next updates. This status could be changed at any moment, only by Cider Collective
