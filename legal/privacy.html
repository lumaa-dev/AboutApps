Privacy Policy
Last Update: March 4th, 2024

Overview

No data is being collected or sent to any third-party.

Threaded collects the least amount of data, no data is sent to first-party servers. Threaded only allows third-party connections and requests with a user-selected Mastodon instance, which may or may not collect your data. Mastodon instances covers these in their Privacy Policy, and RevenueCat for Plus. Threaded does not take responsability for instances' data leaks.

Request to Delete

All on-device sensitive data can be deleted by going on your profile, then tap the 4 bars at the top right, then tap Log out.

Use of Data

The data stored is used to log in to your Mastodon account, which is provided by the user. Your device identifier and payment history is used to identify your Plus payment/subscription and grant you all of its features if you own Plus. Both of the said data is managed by RevenueCat and the app creator

Security Measures

All data received from any of Lumaa's apps to Lumaa's servers are encrypted, and decrypted only under strict conditions. If a security breach ever happens, users will be notified and all data will be erased.

Changes to the Policy

This Privacy Policy is subjected to change at any time, when it occurs, users of apps sending data will be notified and users will be required to accept the Policy.
